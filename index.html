Be Cool about Wildfires

As global warming continues to rise, the frequency and intensity of natural disasters, like wildfires, have significantly increased. Our mission is to connect individuals affected by these fires with local communities willing to provide support. Building strong community connections and offering a helping hand to those in need is our top priority.
