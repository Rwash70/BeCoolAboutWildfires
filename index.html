Be Cool about Wildfires

The global climate crisis has manifested itself in more ways and more frequently than ever before. Wildfires have grown far too common and resources for victims need to meet the need at the source. Our mission is to turn the community itself into a resource by connecting victims to unaffected neighbors willing to lend a cooling hand.
